He sang, and ate, and sang, and ate everyday.
But one day, he thought "Yo, what is up?"
He began to contempate the purpose of his actions.
Then he started pumping his muscles, lifting some weights.
Until he was ripped af. Humberto went from a buff dude who ate
all the time to a buff dude who didn't eat all the time.
To this day, he still retains the original white T-shirt he
wore at the beginning of his journey. Though he still enjoys the sweet
and savory pizza goodness every once in a while.
